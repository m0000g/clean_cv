My love for design

I love drawing since I was a little kid, and being a freelancer open me the doors to apply what I have learned and mix it up with my creativity. The real challenge is to see the big picture and bring to life piece by piece what my client needs withing their limitations. Every project is different but here is a basic work-flow:

  • Analysis and planfication: Defining the problem , goals (requirements), essential dates and limitation of the project.
  • Design: Sometimes starts with just ideas that transform themselves into wire-frames and later on in mock-ups or functional prototypes. And sometimes so small as a simple heuristic evaluation to fix visible design problems.
